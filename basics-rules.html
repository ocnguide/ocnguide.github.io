Rules

General Composure

  1. Maintain a pleasant, casual atmosphere. Be respectful and use common sense. Excessive swearing, rudeness and harassment is not tolerated.
  2. Try to maintain proper spelling and grammar.
  3. Do not spam.
  4. Do not link to any of the following:
    1. Objectionable material, which includes but is not limited to pornography, "shock sites", viruses, phishing sites and hate groups.
    2. Other websites or servers in a manner that constitutes advertising.
    3. Personal identifying information of other users.
  5. Do not encourage or partake in rule breaking on Overcast Network services.
  6. Do not impersonate any member of the Overcast Network staff, or claim to be a staff member.
  7. Report issues or bugs of Overcast Network services to either the forums (http://oc.tc/forums) or to support@oc.tc; exploitation of bugs for any reason other than to demonstrate at the request of a staff member will result in a punishment.

Website Behavior

  1. Do not make topics or posts aiming to insult or provoke other people or groups, and do not promote or encourage others to make such posts.
  2. Do not reply to topics with irrelevant, meaningless, or spammy text/images.
  3. Do not "bump" topics by posting something that does not contribute constructively to the topic.
  4. All personal issues must be dealt with in private. The public forums are not the place for personal conflicts.
  5. Criticism of the work of others, both staff and the larger community, must be delivered with politeness and personal respect.
  6. Do not make topics just to announce that you are quitting the game or the server. Exceptions may be made if you are quitting for a very interesting reason, like you are dying, or sailing around the world.
  7. Announcements about dramatic events befalling other players must include a verifiable source for the information. If we cannot easily verify the story, the topic may be removed. Deliberate hoaxes of this nature are considered very serious infractions.
  8. Do not reply to a topic that you feel is violating the rules, submit a report instead. Intentionally lying in a report is considered an infraction.
  9. Do not abuse HTML in the forums or personal profiles in a way that makes navigation difficult, causes extreme browser lag, or exceeds the preset boundaries.

Server Behavior

  1. Do not send messages that encourage other players into leaving a team or disconnecting from the server.
  2. Unless you are explaining the report process, you may not encourage others to report a specific player.
  3. Please do not publicly accuse any players of hacking. If you believe a player is hacking or breaking any rules report them using the /report command.
  4. You may not use any form of in game chat to solicit. Any account that is determined to be for the sole use of soliciting will be permanently banned without warning.
  5. You may not link to, or solicit players to join, any server public or private that is not part of the Overcast Network in public chat. (You may use /msg command to inform players that request it).
  6. Your username must be appropriate, and you may be asked to use a different account if your username is found to be inappropriate. (This does not count as ban evasion.)

General Gameplay

  1. Players may not abuse glitches. This includes, but is not limited to, placing blocks in areas restricted by the plugin to walk on, creating an "x-ray" effect, and causing yourself to be invincible.
  2. In a Blitz or Ghost Squadron match, players may not prolong a match by avoiding combat and/or hiding in places that are inaccessible or difficult to get to.
  3. Observers may never give tactical information to players or intentionally disrupt gameplay. Tactical information includes, but is not limited to:
    1. Revealing the locations of players
    2. Revealing the ownership of wool in a Capture the Wool map
  4. Please use an appropriate skin - you may be punished for an inappropriate skin.
  5. Do not spawn camp.
    1. Do not "camp" a spawn by waiting next to or in a spawn for newly spawned players.
    2. Do not directly attack any spawn (e.g. run towards a spawn and start hitting people in that spawn, stick around, and kill everyone that spawns).
    3. Do not aim TNT cannons at a spawn or repeatedly drop TNT on a spawn.
