Staff Ranks

Rank Features
Administrator Administrators have full access to operator commands and have full control over the server and website. They have a yellow flair in-game.
Developer These are people who contributed to the development of the Overcast Network.
Senior Moderator Senior moderators have less power than administrators but more than moderators. They have a maroon flair in-game.
Moderator Moderators have access to operator commands have are allowed to punish players for breaking the rules. They have a red flair in-game.
Junior Moderator Junior moderators are inexerienced but are likely to be promoted to moderator status after a period of time. They have similar levels of access to commands as moderators but are not allowed to use WorldEdit and setnext commands. They have a pink flair in-game.
Map Developer Map Developers are skilled map makers who have dedicated servers for map testing as well as access to change to server rotations.
Event Coordinator Event Coordinators are in charge of organizing tournaments.
Supreme Overlord This rank is like the administrator rank given to two of MonsieurApple's friends. They have a maroon flair in-game.
