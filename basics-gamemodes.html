Game Modes

Capture the Wool

The objective of this gamemode is to retrieve the wool stationed in enemy territory and bring it back to base. Both teams will try and do this, so wool defenses are necessary to prevent the enemy from winning.

Destroy the Core/Monument

The objective of this gamemode is to go over to enemy territory and destory the monument/core. Both teams will be trying to do this so defenses are necessary to prevent the enemy from winning.

Team Deathmatch

The objective of this gamemode is for one team to get the most kills in a certain time period.

King of the Hill

The objective of this gamemode is to capture the hill(s), which will increment the team score. The first team to reach a certain score wins.

Blitz

This objective of this gamemode is to eliminate the other team by killing them. There are limited lives in this gamemode.

Rage

This gamemode is more of a meta-gamemode. A Rage map is a map where players are 1 hit kills.

Ghost Squadron

This the goal of this gamemode is to eliminate the other team by killing them while invisible. There are different classes with different abilities in this gamemode.
